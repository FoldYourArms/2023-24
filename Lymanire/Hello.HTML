how to do a toe touch on floor

if you can do it on a trampoline that would help a lot

first you will start by grasping your hands together in front of you in a cone

then you will bring your hands down still together and lift them up to a t-pose

next you will keep a t-pose in the air while your legs are in a middle split in the air

now you will land in a sitting position with your hands under your thighs

lastly you will stand back up clean with your hands by your side
