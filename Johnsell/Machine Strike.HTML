Machine Strike
Welcome fellow Strikers!
This is a how to in case you forget how to play.
This can also help beginning strikers.
Let's start with the pieces.

You can do an overview of the pieces by pressing you left joystick down.
Then you go down to the machines section. This is also where you can find details about the board. We will get to that later.
below is a picture of how to figure out what each card means.

In the bottom left corner of the card, you will see a little tear-drop. This shows a machine's weak points. Blue means strong points and pink means weak points.
Along the bottom of the card, it will show the machines stats. For example, a machine's attack strength, or move speed.
Just below the picture, it shows the special moves. A burrower has none.
In the top right corner, you will see two triangles stuck together. This represents how many victory points a machine will give when killed.
That is enough about the cards. Let's move on to the board.
